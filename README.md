# FUTURE_DS_02
🚀 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

I recently built a dashboard using the Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle) to explore how customer behavior, billing preferences, and service usage impact churn.
📌 Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer retention and churn patterns for an internet service provider. Built using the Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle), it highlights how customer demographics, service usage, and billing preferences influence churn behavior. The goal is to identify actionable insights that can help reduce churn and improve customer loyalty.

🔑 Key Insights

- Churn Rate: Around 26.5% of customers have churned, while 73.5% remain active.
- Service Type Impact: Customers using fiber optic services show higher churn compared to DSL users.
- Payment Methods: Electronic check users account for the largest share of churn, suggesting billing preferences strongly influence retention.
- Streaming Services: Churn is almost evenly split between customers who use streaming services and those who don’t, indicating streaming alone isn’t a strong retention driver.
- Tenure & Support: Longer tenure correlates with lower churn, while lack of tech support is linked to higher churn.
- Charges: The dataset shows a total of $16.06M in TotalCharges and $456K in MonthlyCharges, with higher monthly charges often associated with churn.
- 
📊 Dataset Used

- Source: Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle)
- Size: 7,000+ customer records
- Features: Demographics (gender, senior citizen, partner), services (internet type, streaming, tech support), billing (payment method, paperless billing), and churn status.
- 
🖥️ Dashboard Features
- Interactive Filters: Gender, partner status, phone service, and contract type.
- Visualizations:
- Bar charts for tenure by internet service and churn by streaming movies.
- Donut charts for churn distribution and paperless billing adoption.
- Line chart showing tech support usage across tenure.
- KPIs for total charges, monthly charges, and churn percentage.
- User-Friendly Layout: Clear segmentation of retention vs churn drivers for quick decision-making.

✅ Conclusion

This dashboard demonstrates how customer churn is influenced by service type, billing preferences, and support availability. By focusing on fiber optic customers, electronic check users, and those lacking tech support, businesses can design targeted retention strategies. The insights provide a strong foundation for predictive modeling and proactive customer engagement.
